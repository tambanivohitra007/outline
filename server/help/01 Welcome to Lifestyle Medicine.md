Welcome to the Lifestyle Medicine knowledge base \u2014 a collaborative platform for building evidence-based treatment guides using the NEWSTART+ lifestyle medicine framework.

This help collection walks you through every feature of the platform: creating conditions, managing interventions, collaborating with your team, and publishing treatment guides.

## What you can do here

- **Create treatment guides** for medical conditions, organized into structured sections
- **Link interventions** from the NEWSTART+ care domains (Nutrition, Exercise, Water, Sunlight, Temperance, Air, Rest, Trust in God, and more)
- **Attach evidence** from PubMed and clinical trials to support each intervention
- **Reference Scripture and Spirit of Prophecy** quotations relevant to health
- **Use AI** to generate draft content, explanations, and review summaries
- **Collaborate** with your team through a draft \u2192 review \u2192 publish workflow
- **Export** conditions in FHIR R4 format for interoperability with health systems
