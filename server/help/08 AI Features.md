The platform includes AI-powered tools to help you draft content, generate explanations, and review treatment guides.

## Available AI models

The platform supports three AI providers. Your administrator selects which model the team uses:

| Model | Provider | Best for |
|-------|----------|----------|
| **Gemini 2.0 Flash** | Google | Fast generation, good for drafting |
| **GPT-5.2** | OpenAI | Detailed clinical content |
| **Claude Sonnet 4.6** | Anthropic | Nuanced explanations and analysis |

The active model can be changed in team settings by an administrator.

## AI content generation (Section panels)

Each condition section has an **AI Generate** button. When clicked:

1. The AI generates content tailored to the condition name and section type
2. The generated content appears as a formatted preview below the section
3. You can:
   - **Copy & Edit** \u2014 copies the markdown to your clipboard and opens the document editor
   - **Dismiss** \u2014 closes the preview

### What the AI generates for each section

| Section | AI generates |
|---------|-------------|
| Risk Factors | Known risk factors, predispositions, epidemiological data |
| Physiology | Pathophysiology, disease mechanisms, affected systems |
| Complications | Potential complications, comorbidities, progression risks |
| Solutions | Lifestyle interventions organized by NEWSTART+ domains |
| Bible & SoP | Relevant health principles from Scripture and Ellen G. White |
| Research Ideas | Research gaps, proposed study designs, unanswered questions |

## AI Review Summary

In the condition header, click the **AI Review** button to generate a comprehensive review summary of the entire condition. This analyzes all sections, interventions, evidence, and references to produce:

- An overall assessment of completeness
- Identified gaps in evidence or content
- Suggestions for improvement
- Consistency checks across sections

## In-editor AI tools

When editing any document, you have access to three insert tools:

### Bible verse insert
- Opens a search dialog
- Search by reference (e.g., "Genesis 1:29") or keyword (e.g., "healing")
- Click a result to insert a formatted blockquote into your document

### Spirit of Prophecy insert
- Opens a search dialog for Ellen G. White\u2019s writings
- Search by topic (e.g., "health reform", "diet")
- Click a result to insert a formatted quotation with attribution

### AI explanation
- Enter a topic or leave empty to use the document title
- The AI generates an explanation
- Review the formatted preview
- Click **Insert into document** to add it to your text

## Important notes about AI content

- AI-generated content is a **starting point**, not a finished product
- Always **verify medical claims** against peer-reviewed sources
- **Review and edit** AI content before submitting for review or publishing
- AI may occasionally generate inaccurate information \u2014 cross-reference with PubMed and clinical guidelines
- The AI does not have access to your team\u2019s private documents or patient data
